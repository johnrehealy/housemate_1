# ADR-010: Calendar Is The Visible Layer, Not The Product

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-04-22
Owner: John / Housemate

## Context

Phase 1 centers heavily on Schedule, but the product is not meant to collapse into a generic calendar app.

## Decision

The Phase 1 calendar exists to express the operating plan for the home. The product is not a generic calendar app; the calendar is one manifestation of the household plan of record.

## Why

This protects product strategy and keeps Schedule tied to the real value: helping the household manager stay on top of the home.

## Alternatives Considered

- position the product as primarily a home calendar
- defer the calendar as just one feature among many

## Consequences

Design and implementation decisions should reinforce the operating-plan concept rather than drifting toward generic scheduling software.
