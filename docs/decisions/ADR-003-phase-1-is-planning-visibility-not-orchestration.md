# ADR-003: Phase 1 Is Planning Visibility, Not Orchestration

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-04-22
Owner: John / Housemate

## Context

The broader Housemate vision includes automation, delegated access, and AI orchestration, but Phase 1 needs a sharper and smaller wedge.

## Decision

Phase 1 focuses on establishing the home plan of record, visualizing it in calendar form, setting reminders, and allowing lightweight SMS edits. AI orchestration is explicitly deferred.

## Why

This creates a real first value proposition without dragging the product into premature automation complexity.

## Alternatives Considered

- build the automation story into Phase 1
- build a generic home-management shell with diffuse value

## Consequences

Phase 1 becomes a planning-and-visibility product with a credible path to later automation, rather than a shallow automation promise.
